Why Add a Recording?

To get the most meaning from your gift, I recommend pairing the written poem with a voice recording. Often people become focused on reading the words and may miss some of the emotion and meaning behind them. When they are able to listen while they read, the message has a chance to truly sink in, creating a deeper and more memorable experience.
