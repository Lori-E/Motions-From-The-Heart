Baby Birth Poem – “A New Little Life”

Sample Customer Answers

  • Occasion: Birth of first baby
  • Memorable moment: Parents seeing their baby for the first time
  • Nicknames: (Example: “Peanut”)

Resulting Poem

As the time goes near,
All the questions will start.
A new life is created
With a single beat of a heart.

A beautiful baby,
A joy to your eyes.
The question of what it will be
No longer a surprise.

It may have Daddy’s lips,
It may have Mommy's hair.
But it's all up to you
To teach them what is right and what is fair.

It won't always be easy,
But it will be worth every mile,
When those first words come out
Through their little happy smile.

When the curiosity stage arises
With that spark in their eyes,
Enjoy their innocence in life—
For the time truly flies.

Don’t forget to enjoy each other
With the new life you helped create.
Take one day at a time;
Leave the leaping to fate.

Being great parents isn't easy,
But it will pay off in the end,
When your child can look at you
Not only as a parent—but also as a friend.
