Sample Styles

These short samples show the tone and style of some of my writing. Your piece will be created from your own details and story.

Anniversary – Romantic & Heartfelt

We started with two nervous smiles
and a borrowed Friday night,
now every ordinary morning
feels like something done just right.

Birthday – Fun & Playful

Another year of jokes and grace,
of coffee spills and warm embrace.
If life’s a cake, you’re the sweet part,
the frosting wrapped around my heart.

Memorial – Gentle & Comforting

Your chair sits quiet by the door,
but love still walks across this floor.
In every laugh and every song,
a piece of you still lives along.
