Romantic Poem Sample

The years may pass, the seasons change,
Yet my heart still feels the same.
Through every smile, through every tear,
I'm thankful that you're always near.

You've been my comfort, strength, and guide,
My greatest blessing by my side.
And if I had one wish come true,
I'd spend forever loving you.

Memorial Poem Sample

Though we cannot hold your hand today,
Your memory never fades away.
The love you shared, the lives you touched,
Will always mean so very much.

Though heaven called and you had to go,
Your spirit remains in hearts below.
And through each memory, laugh, and tear,
We feel your presence ever near.

Faith-Based Sample

When life becomes uncertain and the road is hard to see,
God's gentle hand still guides us where we're meant to be.
His grace provides us strength each day,
And lights our path along the way.

Though storms may come and trials appear,
His promises remain sincere.
For every step and every mile,
We're held within His loving smile.
